Dynasty League Constitution

Draft Version 1.1 | Established May 2026

I. Financials & Dues

To ensure long-term league stability, the following financial rules apply:

  • Platform: All funds are held via LeagueSafe with Majority Approval.
  • The 2-Year Rule: All managers must be paid one year in advance. Initial buy-in covers 2026 and 2027.
  • Future Pick Tax: If a manager trades a draft pick for a year not yet paid for, they must immediately pay the dues for that year.

IV. Scoring System

Passing

  • Passing TD: 5 Points
  • Passing Yards: 1 pt per 25 yards
  • Interception: -3 Points

Rushing & Receiving

  • Reception (All): 0.5 PPR
  • TE Premium: Additional +0.5 per reception (Total 1.0)
  • First Downs: +0.5 per Rushing/Receiving 1st Down
  • Fumbles Lost: -2 Points

V. Drafting & Taxi

Startup Draft

Slow Draft (8-hour clock) including Vets and Rookies. 4-round Rookie Drafts will occur annually in subsequent years.

Taxi Squad Rules

  • Duration: 3 Years (Sleeper Setting: 2 Years Experience).
  • Deadline: Week 1 of the Regular Season.
  • Movement: Promotion is a one-way street.

VI. Draft Order & Tiebreakers

Rookie Draft Order

  • Non-Playoff Teams (1-4): Lowest Max Potential Points (Max PF).
  • Playoff Teams (5-10): Final playoff standings.

Tiebreakers

Seeding ties are broken by Total Points For. This rewards managers for active management and superior start/sit decisions throughout the season.

VII. Trades & Transfers

  • Veto Policy: Commissioner only. Reserved for proven collusion.
  • Trade Deadline: Week 13.
  • Roster Dumping: Illegal. Asset liquidation must be done via trade, not drops.

VIII. Waivers & Free Agency

  • Method: FAAB ($1,000 budget).
  • Processing: Wednesday 3:00 AM EST.
  • Sunday Drops: Bench players may be dropped after their game starts; they lock for 24 hours.
